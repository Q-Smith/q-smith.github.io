Core Values

  • Wisdom

    Having confidence in decision-making; good judgment; keeping perspective.
    Wisdom anchors me and keeps me steady when things shift and change.

  • Trust

    A belief in someone or something.
    Our unique traits set us apart -our solidarity unites us. Perfection is not a requirement for my trust. I think people are flawed and make poor decisions when working from places of stress and fear, so I believe in practicing reciprocal grace and forgiveness. Trust is the umbrella for integrity, honesty, authenticity, faith, optimism, and grace.

  • Growth

    Intellectual, emotional, spiritual, and physical development.
    We are always innovating, looking for new ways to shine, solidly perched on past successes, but never satisfied with the status quo. Growth is about the intrinsic desire to become a better version of myself.

  • Curiosity

    Willingness to explore and learn.
    Experience is just the beginning, learning is the key to our success. I love a good evidence base and also deeply value intituon or inner knowing. Being a curious human helps me show up with joy in every season.

  • Accountability

    Fairness; justice; cause and effect.
    Accountability for me fosters better work relationships, improves daily satisfaction, and helps teams work more effectively together. Accountability is about measuring the distance between your intentions and your impact, then taking the necessary steps to close the gap. This requires introspection, honesty, grace, humility, and a sense of personal responsibility to your role in a community and society.
